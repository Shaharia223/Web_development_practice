Welcome! I am a graduate student in Political Science at the University of Dhaka. I also serve as a Master Trainer at SAVE Youth Bangladesh. My primary interest lies in political data analysis, specifically observing political trends through a data-driven approach. I have worked as a Junior Consultant in International Foundation for Electoral Systems(IFES) on the office of Bangladesh Election Commission for giving Electoral Assistance to the commission. I had also worked in Micro-governance Research Initiative (MGR) as a Data Coder, Research Associate and Program Assistant from 2021 to 2025. Since 2021, I have been collecting and analyzing monthly data on political violence in Bangladesh, contributing to projects supported by USAID and UKAID. Additionally, I have enhanced my expertise through training sessions from IFES, INTERNEWS, and USAID. Currently, I am involved in 'Bangladesh Diplomatic Interaction Project' as a Data coder led by Muhib Rahman,a Perry World House Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Pennsylvania.

I am passionate about contributing to governance and politics through my work. I have acquired proficiency in data analysis with Python, and data visualization using Google Looker Studio, IBM Cognos and MySQL. I am dedicated to fostering improvements in Bangladeshi political discourse and the broader international political domain.
